Sander, rev 537

	XMME v.1.0.1: added dgraph of bielefeld-model.xme
	Left-hand side shows labor market interaction between household and firm.
	Right-hand side shows consumption market interaction between household and mall.

	The production cycle starts with production planning (this uses the memory variable equity).
	It ends on the lower RHS with Firm_calc_revenues and Firm_compute_balance_sheet
	This sets the variables to start a new round of financial policy planning.
