\title{System of National Accounts in EURACE}
\author{Sander van der Hoog, Saul Desiderio, Marco Raberto, Andrea Teglio and Philipp Harting}
\date{Final version, October 2009}
\maketitle

\abstract{This document provides an overview of the system of interlinked balance sheets in the EURACE Model.
We set up a system of national accounts and a SAM (Social Accounting Matrix) in which all monetary stock-flow relationships are summarized. This system provides the backbone for the integration of the EURACE Model. In a next step, we provide a list of accounting identities that must always hold for all monetary and physical flows, both in and out of equilibrium.  
Finally, certain time-invariants can be identified that serve as economic conservation rules.}
